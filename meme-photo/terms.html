2. Description of Service

Meme Photo is a Chrome browser extension that enables users to upload images from web pages directly to their Google Photos account via the right-click context menu. The extension:

  • Allows one-click image uploads to Google Photos
  • Lets you organize uploads into albums
  • Displays upload history locally on your device
  • Requires authentication with your Google account

Privacy Policy for Meme Photo

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Meme Photo ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that enables users to upload images from web pages directly to their Google Photos account via the right-click context menu. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Key Point: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Information

When you authorize the extension, we access the following information via Google OAuth:

  • Profile Name: Your Google account display name
  • Profile Photo URL: Your Google account profile picture

This information is used solely to display your account in the extension's user interface.

2.2 Upload History

When you upload an image to Google Photos, we store the following locally:

Data Purpose
Timestamp Display upload time in history
Filename Identify uploaded images
Media Item ID Fetch thumbnails from Google Photos
Product URL Link to view image in Google Photos
Album ID (optional) Track which album the image was uploaded to

2.3 Cached Data

  • Album Names: Cached for 7 days to reduce API calls
  • Thumbnails: Cached locally as Base64 for faster loading
  • Selected Album: Your preferred upload destination

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and access Google Photos on your behalf
  • Image Upload: To upload images you select to your Google Photos account
  • Display History: To show your recent uploads within the extension
  • User Interface: To personalize the extension with your profile information

4. Data Storage

Local Storage Only: All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) on your device. Maximum storage is 10 MB.

We do NOT:

  • Store any data on external servers
  • Transmit your data to third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect browsing history or personal information beyond what is listed above

5. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with the following Google APIs:

API Purpose Scope
Google Photos Library API Upload images and read app-created content photoslibrary.appendonly, photoslibrary.readonly.appcreateddata
Google UserInfo API Get your name and profile photo userinfo.profile

Your data is sent directly to Google's servers following their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Google account password.

6. Permissions Explained

Permission Why We Need It
identity To authenticate with your Google account via OAuth
storage To store upload history and preferences locally
contextMenus To add "Upload to Google Photos" option when right-clicking images
scripting To show upload notifications (Toast messages) on web pages
host_permissions (all URLs) To download images from any website you choose to upload

7. Data Retention

  • Upload History: Kept until you delete it manually (maximum 50 records)
  • Album Cache: Automatically expires after 7 days
  • User Profile: Cleared when you log out
  • Thumbnail Cache: Cleared when you log out

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View: See all stored data in the extension popup
  • Delete: Remove individual upload records or clear all history
  • Revoke Access: Log out from the extension to clear all cached data
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data

9. Security

  • OAuth 2.0 is used for secure authentication with Google
  • We never see or store your Google password
  • All communication with Google APIs uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access tokens are managed by Chrome's identity API

10. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please submit feedback through one of the following methods:

  • Leave a review or question on the Chrome Web Store extension page
  • Open an issue on our feedback repository
